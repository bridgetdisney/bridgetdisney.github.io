Research Interests

Data science education for librarians, data curation (including metadata and dataset discovery), research assistance services, data visualization, text analysis, data mining, and digital humanities.

Possible Roadmap for Future Research

Data science education for librarians:

Several students within the School of Information Science and Learning Technologies have expressed an interest in learning technical skills, namely data science. While there are pockets of data related courses offered within the school, fundamental data science courses offered in different departments are not available to SISLT students.
The research would require determining which data science skills would be most useful in librarires and matching those skills to library courses offered by ALA accredited library science programs. The research question would be: Are library schools meeting the need for data science education? How are outside organizations (e.g. Carpentries) contributing to data science knowledge of librarians?

Dataset Discovery and Reuse:

This could be a case study of five papers, each representing different disciplines, where the author searched for and reused datasets. The specific challenges encountered using and integrating existing data would be covered as well as the advantages. The purpose would be to help research librarians understand how to bests serve patrons with research and data curation needs.

Research Assistance Services:

More and more universities are providing specialiazed research assistance through their libraries. Two examples are The Scholar's Lab at the University of Virginia, and the Center for Digital Research for the Humanities (CDRH) at the University of Nebraska. What strategies have these centers employed and how do they help researchers?

Data Visualization:

What tools are available for data visualization and what are their uses, strengths, and weaknesses?

Text Analysis and Data Mining:

Comparing manual qualitative research pattern analysis to automated analysis using text analysis and data mining techniques.
